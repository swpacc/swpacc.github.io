Donations and Swag!

The SWPACC accepts donations of any amount. You can also purchase SWPACC swag, like our logo t-shirt or logo Silipint pint glass.

Donations and purchases can be done via our Sqaure store.

The climbing communities generous financial contributions donations of time have helped us to do some great work in SWPA and beyond, including replacement of many bolts and anchors at at Lost Crag and Breakneck Rocks, and clean-ups at Beam Rocks and Coopers Rock.
